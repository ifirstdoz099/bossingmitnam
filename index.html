Technological University of The Philippines

The Technological University of the Philippines Visayas (TUPV) is a branch campus of the Technological University of the Philippines (TUP), which is a state university system in the Philippines. TUPV is located in the Visayas region of the country, specifically in Talisay City, Negros Occidental.TUPV is known for its strong emphasis on science and technology education. It strives to produce graduates who are competent, innovative, and socially responsible. The university also promotes research and development activities to contribute to the advancement of knowledge and technology in the region. As part of the larger Technological University of the Philippines system, TUPV upholds the university's mission of providing accessible and quality education to Filipino students.

  • BS in Electronics and Communications Engineering
  • BS in Mechanical Engineering
  • BS in Engineering Technology
  • Computer Engineering Technology
  • Electrical Engineering Technology
  • Mechanical Engineering Technology
  • Chemical Engineering Technology
